Authoring of Copyright Contract

       
Author Name   Registered name. Not used in contract creation or deployment.  
Email   This is the email you have registered & signed in with.  
Eth Address   This is the Eth account you would use. Eth-Gas & Baanda-gas will be charged from this account.  
Eth Balance   Balance in Eth in the account. Contract creation needs sufficient fund for NL to FL trans-creation and SM implemetation gas.  
Gas Required   Minimum gas in Wie needed. 1 Eth = 1000000000000000000 Wei. Plus Baanda-fee for trans-creation.  
Contract Name   Enter contract name, start with upper case alphabet, max size of 15 characters.  
Parent Contract   Parent contract (Softchain). Genesis (default/stand alone); not used in MVP/Demo.  
Contract Intent   Provide a intent of authoring the contract for future reference. It is an optional field.  
Eth Password   Provide password or passphrase of your Eth account you are using to deploy the contract.  
Provider URL   Now, a local Geth client is used. In production, users can use any Eth client. Baanda will init a default.  
Geth Connection Info   This is the geth node you are connected to. If connection problem try (e.g. https://ropsten.infura.io/TOKEN)
